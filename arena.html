How to use

This tool has two modes.: preview and simulator.
If you want to take a look how your arena is doing, all you need to do is insert your building levels into the first column, insert your current runes into the 'Starting runes' field and finally check all speed and rate upgrades you have. Now the tool will show you your current production.
If you want to look how much you need for an upgrade, you can hover above the green buttons. This will show you the potential gain from your upgrades. You can see how much will the upgrade cost and how long it will take for you to afford it in the 'Upgrade Cost' column.
To use the simulator function, you should / can specify following options: Target, which the simulation will reach and end; Upgrade style, that the program will use to upgrade buildings and the Sacrifice style, that tells the program when to sacrifice and start anew.
