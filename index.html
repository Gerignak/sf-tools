How to get the HAR files
  • Open developer tab by F12 and navigate over to Network tab.
  • Now open the game and you should see items starting to appear.
  • If you want to save a player or a guild, simply click on them in Hall of Fame / guild screen.
  • When you are done, click on Save all as HAR and save the file on your computer.
  • To import the file, simply drag it over to this page.
